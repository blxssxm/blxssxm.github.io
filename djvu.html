The DjVU file format

The DjVu file format, is an alternative to .pdf. A .djvu file has a smaller file size than a .pdf with no difference in quality. Converting all your local documents/articles that exist as .pdf into .djvu will save you a shit ton of space in the long run. Howver I wouldn't recommend sharing files to people as .djvu they will most likely get angry at you and think you are sending them a virus and accuse you of wanting to be different
